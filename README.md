TurboFlow is a next-generation Layer-1 designed for perpetual trading — built from the ground up to offer retail traders greater leverage flexibility, lower fees, and smoother execution than institutional platforms.

It’s built entirely around the needs of everyday traders.



## Why We’re Building TurboFlow

Perpetual DEXs are now one of DeFi’s fastest-growing sectors, surpassing $1 trillion in monthly trading volume. Yet retail traders remain underserved — facing limited leverage, high fees, and poor execution compared to institutions. TurboFlow is built to change that.

TurboFlow is created to solve these challenges by building  a trustable and transparent infrastructure that provides retail traders a fair and accessible trading framework. The platform aims to offer the widest leverage range (and as a result, the lowest capital requirements), cheapest fee structures, and friendliest liquidation levels in the industry.<br>

## Key Features

1. **1000x Leverage on Major Tokens**\
   Trade with ultra-high leverage of up to 1000x on popular crypto assets. This enables traders to enter positions with lower capital while maintaining flexible control over leverage.
2. **Optional Profit-Sharing Fee Model**\
   Traders can opt for a profit-sharing model that eliminates upfront fees and slippage. Trades are executed at the oracle price, with no additional cost or slippage, provided the position size is within trading limits. If the trade is profitable, a portion of the profit is shared with the protocol; if not, there is no fee charged. This model offers a smooth and cost-effective trading experience.



By delivering low fees, flexible leverage, and a seamless user experience, Turboflow aims to become the most trader-friendly decentralized perpetual exchange—providing instant trade execution and the highest possible win rates among all platforms.
