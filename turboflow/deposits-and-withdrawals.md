# Deposits & Withdrawals

## Deposits

**Users can freely deposit into TurboFlow using USDC or USDT from either the Solana or BSC (Binance Smart Chain) networks.** The **quote currency** on TurboFlow is **USDT** by default.When deposits are made in **USDC**, the value may reflect a **premium or discount** relative to USDT, based on **real-time market exchange rates**.

All conversions are executed automatically to ensure accurate margin and position valuation.



## Withdrawals

On the **TurboFlow Chain**, bridging in and out is **fully unrestricted**, allowing funds to move freely across the network.\
To ensure the safety of all users:

* Any **daily withdrawal exceeding 10%** of the Vault’s total value will trigger a system alert.
* **Additional withdrawals** beyond this threshold require approval from the chain’s **node operators**.
* Once verified as normal, the **withdrawal quota resets to zero**, restoring unrestricted withdrawal access.



## Node Operator Review Triggers

In addition to withdrawal thresholds, certain activities may trigger **a node operator review and vote** to ensure fair use and system integrity.\
These include, but are not limited to:

* Using **multiple accounts** to bypass trading size limits or profit-sharing structures.
* Engaging in **abnormal trading behaviors** that may affect Vault balance or system stability.



This governance mechanism keeps TurboFlow fair, transparent, and resistant to exploitation.
