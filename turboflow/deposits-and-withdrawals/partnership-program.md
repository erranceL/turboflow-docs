# Partnership Program

TurboFlow’s Partnership Program is designed for agents, affiliates, trading groups, and other growth partners who want to participate in the platform’s expansion over time.

Compared with the standard Referral Program, the Partnership Program is intended for partners with stronger distribution capability, user reach, or relevant trading communities.

### What It May Include

Depending on the partnership type and platform rules, partners may receive support such as:

* enhanced rebate or reward arrangements
* dedicated collaboration support
* access to selected campaigns or growth initiatives
* early visibility into relevant product or marketing opportunities

### Who It’s For

The Partnership Program may be suitable for:

* community leaders
* trading communities
* KOLs and creators
* agents and affiliates
* ecosystem or channel partners

### How to Join

Users or organizations interested in the Partnership Program should contact TurboFlow through [official channels](https://t.me/turboflow_xyz) for the latest application process and eligibility requirements.

### Important Notice

Partnership eligibility, support structure, reward arrangements, and applicable conditions may vary over time.

Users should always refer to the latest product interface and official TurboFlow announcements for the most current information.
