# Affiliate Program

TurboFlow’s Affiliate Program is designed for partners who want to support the ecosystem’s growth through distribution, community reach, content, and ecosystem participation.

Compared with the standard Referral Program, the Affiliate Program is intended for participants with stronger reach, greater growth capability, or more active community influence.

### What It May Include

Depending on the affiliate type and platform rules, participants may receive support such as:

* **enhanced rebate or reward arrangements**
* **dedicated collaboration support**
* **access to selected campaigns or growth initiatives**
* **early visibility into relevant product or marketing opportunities**

### Who It’s For

The Affiliate Program may be suitable for:

* community leaders
* trading communities
* ecosystem or channel partners
* KOLs
* ambassadors

### KOLs

TurboFlow welcomes KOLs, creators, and growth contributors who want to collaborate through content, community reach, and **referral-based promotion**.

KOL participation is generally structured through **performance-based incentive arrangements rather than fixed compensation**. Specific terms may vary depending on the cooperation model and applicable platform rules.

### Ambassadors

TurboFlow also works with ambassadors under deeper collaboration models designed for more sustained ecosystem contribution.

Depending on the role and arrangement, ambassador participation may include **dedicated support, broader campaign involvement, and fixed-support structures** in addition to platform-based incentives.

### How to Join

Users or organizations interested in the Affiliate Program should contact TurboFlow through [official channels](https://t.me/turboflow_xyz) for the latest application process and eligibility requirements.

### Important Notice

Affiliate eligibility, support structure, reward arrangements, and applicable conditions may vary over time.

Users should always refer to the latest product interface and official TurboFlow announcements for the most current information.
