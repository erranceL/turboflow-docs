# INCENTIVES

TurboFlow’s incentives layer is designed to make participation more engaging, more visible, and more rewarding over time.

Rather than relying on a single rewards mechanism, TurboFlow combines multiple incentive formats to support different kinds of user activity across the platform — from trading participation and streak-based rewards to public visibility and growth contributions.

### In this section

#### Points

A platform-wide rewards and participation system designed to reflect user activity across TurboFlow.

#### Leaderboard

A public performance layer that highlights standout activity and makes participation more visible across the platform.

### Why it matters

TurboFlow’s incentives are designed to support more than just rewards distribution.

They help create a market experience that is more active, more social, and more connected — giving retail users more reasons to participate, stay engaged, and grow alongside the ecosystem.
