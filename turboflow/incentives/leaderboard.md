# Leaderboard

TurboFlow Leaderboard is designed to make platform activity more visible and more engaging for users.

It allows users to explore standout performance across the platform and follow how active participation appears over time.

### What You Can See

Depending on the current product interface, the Leaderboard may display information such as:

* top trades
* top traders
* realized PnL
* ROI
* time-based ranking views

Displayed data and ranking views may vary depending on the active product interface and platform updates.

### Why It Matters

The Leaderboard adds a more public and social layer to the TurboFlow experience.

It helps surface standout activity across the platform and gives users another way to track momentum, visibility, and performance.

### Looking Ahead

TurboFlow may introduce more leaderboard-based campaigns, incentives, and community activities over time.

Please refer to official platform announcements for the latest updates and upcoming events.
