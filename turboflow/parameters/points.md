# Points

TurboFlow Points is the platform’s points system, designed to reflect eligible activity across TurboFlow.

### How It Works

Points may include activity related to:

* trading participation
* referrals
* campaigns

The exact allocation logic is determined by platform rules and may vary over time.

### Where to Check

Users can view their total points directly through the Points interface on TurboFlow.

Displayed balances may also include point categories such as airdrop points or other eligible distributions when applicable.

### Update Cycle

Points are updated on a scheduled basis rather than in real time.

Users should always refer to the live product interface for the latest displayed balance, update timing, and applicable notices.

### Important Notice

Points are part of TurboFlow’s platform points system.

They do not by themselves represent cash, tokens, or guaranteed future benefits. Point allocation, update timing, and any related utility remain subject to platform rules and may change over time.
