# Referral Program

Invite friends to trade on **TurboFlow** and earn a share of their trading activity.

* **Flat Fee Mode:**\
  Earn **30% of trading fees** from your invitees by default.
* **Profit Sharing Mode:**\
  Earn **0.5 basis points (bp)** of effective trading volume.\
  Effective trading volume is defined as any trade with an underlying asset price movement greater than **0.2%**, ensuring fair and anti–wash-trade rewards.



Your rebate rate increases as your invitees’ cumulative trading volume grows:
