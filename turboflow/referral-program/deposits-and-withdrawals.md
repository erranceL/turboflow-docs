# Deposits & Withdrawals

#### Supported Networks

TurboFlow currently supports deposits using **USDT** and **USDC** through the following external networks:

* **Solana**
* **BNB Chain**

Available networks may change over time. Users should always confirm the supported network and asset combination on the latest platform interface before depositing.

#### Deposits

Users can deposit USDT or USDC into TurboFlow through supported external networks.

The quote currency on TurboFlow is **USDT** by default. When deposits are made in USDC, balances may reflect a premium or discount relative to USDT based on real-time market exchange rates. All conversions are handled automatically to support margin accuracy and position valuation.

#### Withdrawals

Deposits and withdrawals remain generally available through the supported networks and asset flows shown on the platform.

To protect users and maintain system integrity, certain withdrawal thresholds or behaviors may trigger additional review.

#### Timing & Security

Most deposits and withdrawals are processed through the platform’s normal flow.

In some cases, processing times may take longer than usual due to network conditions, bridge congestion, security checks, or additional review triggered by platform risk controls. This does not necessarily indicate an issue with the transaction itself.

These review procedures are designed to help protect user assets, maintain system integrity, and reduce the risk of abuse or abnormal activity.

Users should always refer to the latest platform interface and official support channels for the most up-to-date transaction status and handling conditions.

#### Review Triggers

A system alert may be triggered when daily withdrawal activity exceeds defined thresholds relative to vault size.

Additional review may also apply in cases involving:

* unusual withdrawal activity
* multi-account abuse
* attempts to bypass trading rules
* abnormal trading behavior that may affect vault balance or system stability

#### Important Notice

To maintain fairness, transparency, and platform integrity, TurboFlow may apply risk controls, review procedures, or temporary restrictions where necessary.

Users should always rely on the latest platform interface and official documentation for currently supported assets, networks, and withdrawal conditions.
