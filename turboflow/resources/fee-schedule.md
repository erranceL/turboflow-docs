# Fee Schedule

TurboFlow’s fee treatment may vary depending on the product, trading model, and market conditions.

Users should always refer to the live product interface for the latest applicable values. This page provides a general reference for the current fee structure.

### Perpetual Trading

#### Flat-Fee Model

Under the Flat-Fee Model, most assets fall within the 4–6 basis point (bp) trading fee range, where 1 bp = 0.01%.

Opening and closing fees are charged upfront when a position is opened. No additional fee is charged again at closing.

#### Slippage

Slippage is calculated as:

position size / price slippage factor + base slippage

The latest slippage-related values should be confirmed through the live product interface or applicable reference tools.

#### Profit-Sharing Model

Under the Profit-Sharing Model, no fee or slippage is charged at entry.

If a trade closes in profit, the platform charges a share of that profit according to the applicable model rules. If a trade closes in loss, no fee or slippage applies.

### Funding Fees

Funding fees are charged on a scheduled basis depending on the token and applicable market conditions.

* When funding is positive, longs pay shorts
* When funding is negative, shorts pay longs

Under the Profit-Sharing Model, the benefiting side receives 70% of the effective funding amount.\
Under the Flat-Fee Model, the benefiting side receives 100% of the effective funding amount.
