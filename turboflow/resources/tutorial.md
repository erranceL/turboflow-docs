# Tutorial

## Perpetual Trading

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### **Before You Begin Trading**

TurboFlow enables seamless deposits and withdrawals across the **Solana** and **BNB Chain** networks.\
It is also compatible with popular wallet solutions such as **MetaMask, OKX Wallet** and **Phantom**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For the purpose of this tutorial, we will demonstrate using **OKX Wallet** as an example.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### **Logging In**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (12).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Visit the **TurboFlow** website and click on **“Log In.”**\
You can choose to log in either with your **email** or by **connecting your wallet address**.\
If you choose the wallet login option, **TurboFlow** supports integration with multiple wallet providers.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### **Dashboard**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (13).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The Dashboard serves as the central hub for managing your TurboFlow account.

It provides a clearer overview of your account profile, wallet balances, deposit and withdrawal access, trading volume, and position-related information.

From the Dashboard, you can:

* view your nickname, account type, account details, and user ID
* check your trading volume
* view your Spot Asset balances
* quickly access Deposit and Withdraw
* monitor your Perps Positions
* access transaction and account history from the left-side menu

Users should always rely on the live product interface for the latest available dashboard functions and account management options.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### **Deposit to Your TurboFlow Account**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (14).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

From the Dashboard, click Deposit in the left-side menu to open the deposit page.

Then follow these steps:

1. **Select Coin**\
   Choose the asset you want to deposit.
2. **Select Network**\
   Choose the network you want to use for the transfer.
3. **Copy Deposit Address**\
   Copy the address generated by TurboFlow and use it to send funds from your external wallet or exchange account.

Before sending, **make sure the selected asset and network match exactly**. Users should always refer to the live product interface for the latest supported deposit options and requirements.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### **Trading**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (15).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



Once your funds are available in TurboFlow, you can begin trading from the Trade page.

The trading panel allows users to configure the key parts of an order before opening a position.

#### Opening a Trade

Before placing an order, review the following settings in the order panel:

* **Order Type**\
  &#xNAN;_&#x43;hoose the order type supported by the current interface, such as Market or Limit._
* **Position Direction**\
  &#xNAN;_&#x43;hoose whether you want to open a Long or Short position._
* **Collateral Asset**\
  &#xNAN;_&#x53;elect the supported collateral asset available in your account balance._
* **Order Size**\
  &#xNAN;_&#x45;nter the amount you want to use for the position._
* **Leverage**\
  &#xNAN;_&#x53;et leverage according to the selected market and the options currently shown in the live interface._

After reviewing these settings, confirm the order through the trading panel.

#### Closing a Position

Open positions can be managed from the Positions section.

Depending on the current interface, available close actions may include:

* **Market Close** — closes the position immediately at the current market price
* **Limit Close** — closes the position when the selected price is reached
* **Reverse Position** — closes the current position and opens a new one in the opposite direction

Users should always review market conditions, position size, and available controls before choosing a closing method.

#### Important Notes

* Supported order options may vary by market and interface version
* Available collateral assets and leverage settings depend on the live product interface
* High leverage increases both opportunity and risk
* Users should always confirm order details carefully before submitting a trade
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## Event Contracts

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Before You Begin

Before participating in Event Contracts, make sure you have logged in to TurboFlow and have available balance in your account.

Event Contracts are short-cycle, direction-based market products. Users should always refer to the live product interface for currently supported markets, durations, and displayed return rates.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Open the Events Page

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

From the top navigation bar, click **Events** to enter the Event Contracts interface.

This page is where users can select markets, choose contract durations, and participate in short-cycle directional trading.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Choose a Market and Duration

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (17).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After entering the Events page, select a supported market such as **BTC/USDT** or **ETH/USDT** as highlighted in red in the screenshot above.

Then choose a contract duration. TurboFlow currently supports multiple short-cycle windows, including:

* 30 sec
* 1 min
* 3 min
* 5 min
* 15 min
* 1 hour

Users should always rely on the live interface for the latest available markets and durations.

Display preferences can also be adjusted through **Effects Settings** if needed.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Enter an Amount

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (18).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Enter the amount you want to use for the contract.

TurboFlow may also provide preset amount buttons to help users choose quickly. Before confirming, review your available balance, displayed return rate, and estimated profit shown in the order panel.

Please note that the maximum entry amount may vary depending on the selected contract duration. Under the current interface:

* **30 sec / 1 min:** lower max limits
* **3 min / 5 min:** higher max limits
* **15 min / 1 hour:** highest max limits currently available

_These limits may be adjusted over time based on market conditions and platform risk management. Users should always refer to the live product interface for the latest applicable amount limits._
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Choose a Direction

Choose one of the two directions:

* **Higher** — you expect the settlement result to be higher than the entry reference
* **Lower** — you expect the settlement result to be lower than the entry reference

Once selected, confirm the contract according to the current platform interface.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Wait for Settlement

Your Event Contract will settle automatically when the selected time window ends.

Users can view details such as **Enter Price, Exit Price, and settlement outcome** directly in the platform interface. If the final result matches the selected direction under platform rules, the displayed payout is returned. If the settlement result is unchanged, the principal is returned.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Track Market Activity

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can monitor activity and results through the Event Contracts interface, including:

* Public Trades
* Positions
* History
* Leaderboard

Depending on product settings, users may also adjust chart and display options such as Public Feed, Entry Lines, and settlement reveal effects.

#### _\*Important Notes_

* _Return rates may vary by market and duration_
* _Supported markets and settlement windows may change over time_
* _Users should always rely on the live product interface for the latest displayed values_
* _Event Contracts involve high-risk trading activity and should be used responsibly_
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
