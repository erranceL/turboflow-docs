# Tutorial

## **Before You Begin Trading**

TurboFlow enables seamless deposits and withdrawals across the **Solana** and **BNB Chain** blockchain networks.\
It is also compatible with popular wallet solutions such as **OKX Wallet**, **Phantom**, and **MetaMask**.

For the purpose of this tutorial, we will demonstrate using **OKX Wallet** as an example.



## **Logging In**

Visit the **TurboFlow** website and click on **“Log In.”**\
You can choose to log in either with your **email** or by **connecting your wallet address**.\
If you choose the wallet login option, **TurboFlow** supports integration with multiple wallet providers.

## **Dashboard**

The **Dashboard** serves as the central hub for managing your TurboFlow account.\
It provides functionality for **deposits**, **withdrawals**, and an **overview of your account activities**.

From the Dashboard, you can:

* Deposit or withdraw funds from your TurboFlow Spot Wallet.
* View the total balance in your TurboFlow Spot Wallet.
* Manage and monitor all your perpetual trading positions.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>





## **Deposit to Your TurboFlow Spot Wallet**

1. Click on **“Deposit”** on the dashboard.
2.  Choose between **“Direct Deposit”** or **“Cross-Chain Deposits.”**

    > _Note: If you are logged in via email, only the Direct Deposit option is available._

    * **Direct Deposit:**\
      Generates a unique wallet address for you to deposit directly into your TurboFlow Spot Wallet.
    * **Cross-Chain Deposits:**\
      Allows you to deposit funds into your TurboFlow account from your connected wallet across supported networks.
3. **Select a network:**\
   Currently, TurboFlow supports deposits on **SOLANA** and **BSC** networks.
4. **Select the coin you want to deposit.**
5. **Deposit process:**
   * **Direct Deposit:** Copy your unique TurboFlow wallet address and transfer the selected coin.\
     Wait a few minutes for the transaction to be confirmed on-chain, after which your funds will appear in your account.
   * **Cross-Chain Deposit:** Enter the deposit amount, click **Deposit**, and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

## **Trading**

### **Opening a Trade**

* **Order Type**
  * **Market Order:** Executes immediately at the current market price.
  * **Limit Order:** Executes at your specified price.
* **Open/Close Option**\
  Click the **“Open Position”** button.
* **Select Collateral Asset**\
  Supported collateral includes **USDC**, **USDT**, and **Voucher trial funds**.\
  Choose an asset with sufficient balance and enter the desired amount.
* **Leverage Setting**\
  Some tokens support leverage of up to **1000x**.
* **Select Position Direction**
  * **Long:** Expecting the price to rise.
  * **Short:** Expecting the price to fall.\
    High leverage amplifies both potential profits and risks.

### **Closing a Position**

In the **positions list**, you’ll find **Market Close**, **Limit Close**, and **Reverse Position** options next to each position.

* **Market Close:** Instantly closes your position at the current market price.
* **Limit Close:** Sets a specific price at which your position will automatically close.
* **Reverse Position:** Closes the current position and opens a new position of the same size in the opposite direction.

Carefully consider market volatility and your profit targets when choosing a closing method.
