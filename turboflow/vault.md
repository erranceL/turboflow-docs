# Vault

## **Vault Mechanism**

The **Vault** is a community-owned asset pool that acts as the counterparty to all traders on TurboFlow.

* **When traders win:** Payouts are made from the Vault—net of profit-sharing or trading fees.
* **When traders lose:** Their losses accrue entirely to the Vault.
* **All liquidation fees** are also directed to the Vault, allowing it to grow organically alongside platform activity.

As user participation increases, the Vault expands its asset base and trading capacity, reinforcing TurboFlow’s long-term sustainability and scalability.
