# 1000x Leverage

TurboFlow offers up to **1000x leverage** on supported markets, allowing traders to open positions with lower upfront capital and greater flexibility in how they deploy risk.

For retail users, this means market access can become more **capital-efficient**. High leverage allows traders to express short-term conviction with less capital locked upfront, making participation more accessible and more flexible across different trading styles.

But high leverage only matters when the underlying market experience remains fair, understandable, and reliable. TurboFlow is designed to support this through **transparent pricing, flexible trading models, and platform-level risk controls**.

### Built for More Than Just a Bigger Number

TurboFlow’s leverage experience is supported by several layers of product and market design:

#### Transparent Pricing

TurboFlow sources prices from a weighted average of leading exchanges, including Binance, OKX, Bybit, and others. This is designed to improve pricing accuracy and reduce reliance on any single venue.

#### Flexible Trading Models

Traders can choose between different trading models depending on their preferences.

* **Flat-Fee Model**: trading costs are charged upfront and reflect both fees and execution impact
* **Profit-Sharing Model**: no fees or slippage are charged at entry, and the platform only takes a share when the trade closes in profit

This gives users more flexibility in how they approach cost, execution, and position management.

#### Market-Linked Funding

Funding fees are derived from market-implied funding rates on major exchanges and are charged on a scheduled basis depending on the token. This helps keep the system connected to broader market conditions.

#### Risk Controls and Liquidation Logic

TurboFlow applies maintenance margin rules across positions and adjusts risk handling based on leverage level, market conditions, and token-specific parameters. This is intended to support a more structured and transparent liquidation process under high-leverage conditions.

#### Auto Deleveraging Protection

For supported markets, TurboFlow also uses an auto deleveraging mechanism when unrealized profit exceeds the configured cap for a trading pair. This is designed to help manage extreme outcomes and maintain system stability.

### Why It Matters

1000x leverage is not just a headline feature. It reflects TurboFlow’s broader approach to market access: **making powerful trading tools available to everyday users while supporting that experience with clearer rules, transparent pricing logic, and risk-aware design.**

_High leverage increases both opportunity and risk. Users should trade responsibly and ensure they understand the product before participating._
